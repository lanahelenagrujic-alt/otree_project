Instructions

Welcome to the experiment! Please read these instructions carefully.

Experiment Overview

You will participate in {{ C.NUM_ROUNDS }} rounds. In each round:

  • You will be grouped with {{ C.OTHER_PLAYERS }} other participant(s).
  • You must choose between two investment options: Option A or Option B.
  • One option will be randomly selected as successful for the round for each group.
  • You will receive recommendation from an Advisor before making your choice.
    (The Advisor can be correct or incorrect, and you may choose to follow or ignore the advice)
  • You will see the selections made by previous members of your group, but not the advice they received.

Decision Making

  • Decisions are made sequentially, for example:
    • Player 1 decides first without seeing any prior selections.
    • Player 2 can see Player 1 selection before deciding.
    • Player 3 can see Players 1 and Players 2 selections before deciding, and so on...
  • Before making your choice, you will receive a suggestion from the Advisor (e.g., Option "A" or "B").
  • Advisor gives different suggestions to different players even within the same group (e.g., Player 1 may get suggested "A" while Player 2 can get suggested "B", etc.).
  • The suggestion can be correct or incorrect depending on the Advisor's accuracy ‚Äî you may choose to follow or ignore it.
  • If you are not the first player in your group, you will also see the choices made by previous group members before making your own decision.
  • You may:
    • Follow the suggestion you receive from the Advisor
    • Follow choices made by previous group members
    • Use your own independent judgment
    • Or combine any of these approaches

Results

After all decisions are made, you will see:

  • Which option was successful for the round.
  • Whether your selection was correct.
  • Whether you followed/aligned with the Advisor's recommendation.
  • Your earnings for that round.
  • Your total earnings so far.

Your final payment will be the sum of your earnings across all {{ C.NUM_ROUNDS }} rounds.
